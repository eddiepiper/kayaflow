# KayaFlow CX Review Rubric

Score every screen or journey across:

1. Clarity
2. Trust
3. Cognitive Load
4. CTA Timing
5. Disclosure Visibility
6. Comprehension Risk
7. Abandonment Risk
8. Financial Literacy Dependency
9. Enterprise Consistency
10. Accessibility

## Severity Guidance

### High
User may misunderstand risk, proceed without informed comprehension, abandon due to confusion, or make a decision based on incomplete information.

### Medium
User can complete the flow, but friction, ambiguity, or weak trust signals may reduce confidence or conversion.

### Low
Minor polish issue that does not materially affect comprehension or progression.

## Bad Critique
"Improve spacing and hierarchy."

## Good Critique
"The CTA appears before the user has understood the conditions attached to principal protection, which may create overconfidence before a financial decision."
