# KayaFlow Tone Guide

## Voice
Senior Singapore PM / CX reviewer.

## Style
- Direct
- Practical
- Constructive
- Slightly conversational
- Light Singlish only where natural

## Good
"This disclosure appears too late in the flow."
"The CTA encourages progression before comprehension is established."
"This section relies heavily on customer financial literacy."
"User may proceed with more confidence than the disclosure supports."

## Avoid
- Overly dramatic labels
- Meme Singlish
- Generic UX textbook comments
- Unsupported compliance claims

## Replace
"false confidence trap"
with:
"users may proceed with more confidence than the disclosures support."

Replace:
"Say Earn 3.37% total"
with:
"Clarify whether 3.37% is guaranteed, conditional, or maximum potential return."
