# Cognitive Load

## Principles
- Chunk complex information
- Reduce competing priorities on one screen
- Avoid forcing users to compare too many conditions mentally
- Make the next action obvious
- Avoid mixing product education, risk disclosure, and commitment CTA without structure

## Senior Reviewer Checks
- Is the screen asking the user to learn, decide, or act?
- Are too many decisions happening at once?
- Is technical content simplified enough?
- Are numbers explained in context?
