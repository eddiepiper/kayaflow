# Wealth Product UX

## Review Focus
- Product comprehension
- Return ambiguity
- Risk acknowledgement
- Principal protection conditions
- Maturity and holding period conditions
- Early withdrawal risk
- Customer financial literacy burden

## Common Anti-Patterns
- Benefit-first framing without nearby conditions
- "Up to" returns without explanation
- Principal shown without protection condition
- Payout chart without downside explanation
- CTA before customer understands product mechanics

## Good UX Patterns
- Explain product in plain language first
- Place risk explanation near the relevant benefit
- Use comprehension gates before commitment
- Show what is guaranteed, what is conditional, and what is variable
- Provide contextual help beside technical terms
