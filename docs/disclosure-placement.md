# Disclosure Placement

## Rules
- Disclosures should be close to the claim they qualify
- Footer-only disclosure is weak for important financial conditions
- Use inline disclosure anchors for complex claims
- Repeat critical risk near CTA if the CTA advances commitment
- Legal disclosure should support, not replace, plain-language explanation

## Anti-Patterns
- Risk copy below the fold
- Important condition only visible after scrolling
- Disclosure detached from the relevant number or benefit
- Technical legal text used as the only explanation
